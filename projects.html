Turing Completeness, Logic Gates, and the Universality of Conway's Game of Life

This work was done at the LSE for my Master's Dissertation in Mathematics which recieved a distinction. Here I explored the fundamental question of which problems have computable solution using the famous cellular automata, Conway's Game of Life. By building a Turing Equivalent system within Conway's Game of Life I prove that the cellular automata is capable of universal computation. My approach starts with basic building blocks and proving the mathematical equivalences of these blocks to build the complete proof.

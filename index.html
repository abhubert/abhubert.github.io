My name is Aleksander and for work I do math and science on data. For fun I make music, tinker, and walk around the city.

My main interests in data are computer vision, graphics, cognition, and the structure and systems of information and communication. Currently I live in Atlanta working for R-DEX Systems building and analyzing custom machine vision models and exploring the structure of signal and vision data. Before moving back to Atlanta I worked for A/O Proptech in London modeling and analyzing investments in new and emerging technology.

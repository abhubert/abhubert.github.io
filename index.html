About

I am Aleksander a machine learning engineer and software developer with a background in mathematics. I have a passion for exploring the intersection of mathematical thinking and software craft. My work focuses on applying mathematical tools and thinking to solve complex problems with a particular focus on networks, communication systems, and graph theory.

Beyond my professional interests, I enjoy biking around the city, making music, and engaging in creative projects that blend art and technology. I am particularly interested in older techonological contraints and how they influenced the problem solving approaches of the time.
